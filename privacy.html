Last updated: January 15, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to The Unfiltered Trail ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

  • Newsletter Subscription: Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and message content when you contact us
  • Comments: Name, email address, and comment content if we implement a comment system

Information Automatically Collected

  • Analytics Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, time spent on site

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you our newsletter and travel updates (with your consent)

  • Comply with legal obligations
